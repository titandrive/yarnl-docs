# Features

Yarnl is a self-hosted pattern manager built for crocheters who want full control over their projects and data.

---

## Self Hosted

Host Yarnl on your own server and keep complete control of your data.

- **Free forever** — No subscriptions, no premium tiers
- **Your data stays yours** — Private and secure on your own hardware
- **Fully portable** — Export and migrate anytime

---

## Pattern Library

Keep your entire pattern collection organized and searchable.

- **Bulk import** — Add multiple patterns at once
- **Categories & hashtags** — Organize however works for you
- **Smart sorting** — Patterns automatically organized by category
- **Search & filter** — Find patterns by keyword, hashtag, status, or type
- **Quick actions** — Mark favorites or set current project with one click

---

## Pattern Viewer

A distraction-free reading experience with built-in tools to track your progress.

- **Row counters** — Create multiple counters with custom titles (one for the body, another for the sleeves)
- **Timer** — Track how long you spend on each project
- **PDF support** — Your reading position is always saved

---

## Pattern Creation

Write your own patterns directly in Yarnl.

- **Full Markdown support** — Format with headers, lists, tables, and more
- **Clean & portable** — Plain text that works anywhere

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Remove friction and stay in your flow.

- **Fully customizable** — Set up shortcuts that work for you
- **Common actions** — Increment counters, start/stop timer, page forward/back
- **Bluetooth support** — Works with remotes and rings
- **Mobile friendly** — Swipe gestures on touch devices

---

## Customizable UI

Make Yarnl your own with an extensive set of customization options.

- **Themes** — Dark and light mode with auto-switching
- **Fonts** — Choose the typeface that's easiest on your eyes
- **Mascots** — Because why not?
- **And more** — Nearly every part of the UI can be tweaked

---

## Backups

Never lose your progress.

- **Complete data persistence** — Counters, timers, PDF positions, and settings
- **Manual or scheduled** — Back up on demand or automatically
- **Full restore** — Bring back everything exactly as it was
- **Pushover notifications** — Get notified when backups complete or fail

---

## Stats

See your crafting journey at a glance.

- **Time per pattern** — How long you've spent on each project
- **Total time** — Your overall crocheting hours
- **Pattern count** — How many patterns you've tackled
