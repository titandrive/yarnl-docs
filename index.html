Self-Hosted

Host Yarnl on your own server. Free forever with no subscriptions. Your data stays private and fully portable.

Pattern Library

Bulk import patterns, organize with categories & hashtags, and find anything instantly with search and filters.

Pattern Viewer

A distraction-free crocheting experience with everything you need and nothing you don't.

Backups

Automatic backups that save all of your data. No more worrying about losing your place or favorite pattern again.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Fully customizable keyboard shortcuts for counters, timer, navigation, and more. Yarnl also supports Bluetooth controllers.

Customizable UI

Nearly every part of the UI is customizable. Choose from tons of themes and fonts. You can even choose your favorite mascot.

Focus on your craft, not your tools

View your patterns with a built-in PDF reader, track rows with a tap, and pick up exactly where you left off, even weeks later.

  • Row counters. Make as many as you need.
  • Add notes. With full Markdown support.
  • Timer keeps track of your crocheting
  • Custom PDF viewer made just for patterns.
